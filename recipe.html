Classic Tuna Mayo

Recipe description
Nothing mouth watering like the Toasted Tuna Mayo
Ingredients needed:
  • 2 x 170 g cans light meat tuna chunks
  • Three thick mayonnaise packs, plus extra for spreading
  • One cup lemon juice
  • Two branches of Italian parsley, chopped
  • One cuponion, finely chopped
  • Finely chopped celery
  • Two garlic cloves, crushed (optional)
